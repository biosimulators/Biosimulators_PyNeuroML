About BioSimulators-pyNeuroML¶

License¶

This package is released under the MIT License. jNeuroML and pyNeuroML are released under the LGPL 3.0 License. NetPyNE is released under the MIT License. NEURON is released under the BSD-3-Clause.

Development team¶

This package was developed by the Karr Lab at the Icahn School of Medicine at Mount Sinai and the Center for Reproducible Biomedical Modeling. jNeuroML and pyNeuroML were developed by Padraig Gleeson at the University College London. NetPyNe was developed by the Neurosim Lab at SUNY Downstate Medical Center. NEURON was developed by the NEURON Team at Yale University.

Questions and comments¶

Please contact the BioSimulators Team with any questions or comments about the command-line program or Docker image. Please contact the jNeuroML/pyNeuroML, NetPyNe, and NEURON teams about questions about those tools.
